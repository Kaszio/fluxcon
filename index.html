About CypherPunk 2024

Cypherpunk 2024 in Nashville, powered by Flux, is set to be an exciting prelude to the BTC24 conference. This vibrant get-together event will bring together enthusiasts, innovators, and thought leaders from the blockchain and cryptocurrency communities. Admission is free, but registration is required to ensure a spot at this exclusive gathering. Attendees will enjoy delicious appetizers and access to an open cash bar, with two drink tickets included upon registration. Adding to the lively atmosphere, a live band will provide entertainment, making Cypherpunk 2024 an unmissable kickoff to the BTC24 festivities in Nashville.
